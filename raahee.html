Key Features

  • Authentication and Role-Based Access Control
    Implemented secure authentication protocols and role-based access control to safeguard sensitive data and ensure that only authorized personnel can access specific functionalities, enhancing overall data security and compliance.
  • Seamless Data Management for Every Aspect of the Business
    Streamlined operations by efficiently managing data related to customers, raw materials, inventory, products, suppliers, and orders, ensuring smooth workflows and informed decision-making at every step of the process.
  • Automated Conversion of Units for Raw Materials
    Seamlessly converted units of measurement for raw materials, eliminating manual errors and optimizing inventory management processes for greater accuracy and efficiency.
  • Automated Order Processing and Inventory Management
    Implemented automated systems for order processing and inventory management, reducing turnaround times and enhancing overall operational efficiency while minimizing human error.
  • Low Stock Threshold Alerts
    Generated timely alerts when inventory levels fall below predefined thresholds, enabling proactive decision-making and preventing stockouts to maintain uninterrupted business operations.
  • Raw Materials Purchase Price History Tracking
    Enabled tracking the purchase price history of raw materials over time, facilitating cost analysis and strategic procurement decisions to optimize expenses and maximize profitability.
  • Inventory Auditing
    Developed features so that stakeholders can conduct regular inventory audits to ensure accuracy and compliance, identifying discrepancies and minimizing the risk of stock discrepancies or shrinkage.
  • Order Lifecycle Management
    Handled the entire order lifecycle seamlessly from order placement to fulfillment, ensuring smooth order processing, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction.
  • Highly Customizable Product Constituent Configurations
    Integrated constituents to configure products with customizable constituent options for automated inventory deduction and reverts, providing flexibility to adapt to changing market demands and customer preferences with ease.
